I Do Everything

I’m a designer with a background in radio and television journalism. As a journalist, I aim to identify problems through observation of daily life and communication with various kinds of people. As a designer, my goal is to solve problems neatly and elegantly to improve user experience. I enjoy changes and embrace challenges. I work with passion as well as patience, hoping to use my skills to create more innovative and human-centered design that will fulfill not only recent goals but also has

a long-term positive influence on society and environment. But…I’m not only a designer! I’m a photographer who is keen to capture the beauty of life. I’m a music lover who is actively practicing singing and piano. I’m an actor who won the first prize of Shakespeare’s Play Contest. I am an individual who is passionate and curious about nearly everything in life.
I look forward to meeting you and exploring a wider world with you!
